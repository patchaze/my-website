## The Unique Needs of Digital Nomads

Applying for a Schengen visa as a digital nomad or freelancer requires a uniquely tailored approach:
* **Complex Proof of Income:** Instead of a standard corporate NOC (No Objection Certificate), you must present tax returns, client contracts, and complex bank statements.
* **Long-Stay Strategy:** You need to maximize the 90/180-day Schengen rule without triggering tax residency in a specific country.
* **Flexible Bookings:** Nomads rarely have fixed return dates, making the requirement for return flight reservations highly problematic.
